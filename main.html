I’m really interested in the ways that curiosity can affect and shape us. And the pervasive role that the web plays in our discoveries. Prompting a passion for enabling us to transform our ability to create, communicate, and pursue learning. I’m a full stack developer who arrived at tech via fine art. From my studio practice, I’m not unfamiliar with utilizing research to develop my own methods of technique to complete a task from scratch, employing unconventional thinking to solve novel obstacles, and approaching the project at hand from multiple aspects due to a medium’s affect on delivery. My initial and most persistent interest in the field has been in its essence as a tool in the purest, most malleable, and most interpretative form. So now at the ever heightening level of human complexity that we find ourselves in, composite interests in education, accessibility, and transformation when paired with an action bias will allways necessitate tech. Programming has become an essential, precious form of literacy; always at the frontier, as a vividly defining asset for humanity.
