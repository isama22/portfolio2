I’m really interested in the ways that curiosity can affect and shape us. And the pervasive role that the web plays in our discoveries. Prompting us to transform our ability to create, communicate, and pursue learning.

I’m a full stack developer who arrived at tech via fine art. From my studio practice, I’m familiar with utilizing research to complete a task from scratch, employing unconventional thinking to solve novel obstacles, and approaching the project at hand from multiple aspects due to the effect that mediums have on delivery. My initial and most persistent interest in programming has been in its essence as a tool in the purest, most malleable, and most interpretative form. Programming has now become an essential, precious form of literacy; composite interests in education, accessibility, and transformation when paired with an action bias will allways necessitate tech.
