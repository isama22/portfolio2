I'm a full stack software developer with a background in fine art, interested in aiding better technical ability in the field through programming.

•

Out of the four main stem fields, tech is the most humanist. It's the one most shared and accessed by everyone; forever necessitating creativity, being inherently collaborative, and made to be accessable to those who don't even need to know how to write it. I’m fascinated with the implicit “how” of the planning, execution, improvement, and problem solving needed by it. From my studio practice, I know how a medium can undeniably change the expression of a statement. And from that, I have a desire to contribute to the creation of new tools that allow people to express themselves, through the many exciting ways that they can exist today.

•

Based in Los Angeles, I graduated from The School of the Art Institute of Chicago in 2019 with a BFA in studio, and completed General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive this past spring.

Javascript • HTML • CSS • Python • Django • React • MongoDB • Node.js • Express • JWT • AWS S3 • postgreSQL.
